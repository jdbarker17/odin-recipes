Detroit Deep Dish

Well well well. You want pizza huh? Kinda seems to be all the rage nowadays. You're trapped at home during COVID, and well, might as well start making sourdoughs and pizza so Bob nexdoor doesnt try one-upping you at next years Fourth of July neighborhood picnic. This recipe aint gonna be perfect, but Detroit Pizza is the shit. I'm a native Chicagoan, and this pizza has made me question my loyalty.

    Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Sauce
  • Water
  • Any other shit you wana throw on there

    Recipe

  1. Make the pizza dough. Mix yeast and water. Dont kill the sucker.
  2. Mix the flour into the proofed yeast
  3. Wait a long ass time
  4. Shape into pizza shapes
  5. Top with Le Saucy Boy
  6. Top with cheese and pepperoni
  7. Fuck your vegetables
  8. Devour
