Schwarma in the Motor City

Schwarma has to be one of the most predominant foods that I have seen throughout my time here in the Mitten. Dearborn (neighboring town about 20 minutes over) has the second highest concentraition of Arabic people in the world, outside of the Middle East of course. All my coworkers have told me this, so if its wrong, blame those guys. One of my favorite parts of cultural diversity in the area, is the wide variety of traditional food that you get to try. Schwarma is one of those dishes. So unbutton your pants and get ready to smash some Schwarma.

    Ingredients

    Now take this with a grain of salt. Because I'm a white guy that knows little to nothing about making schwarma
  • Chicken Breast/Thigh
  • Pita Bread
  • Garlic Sauce
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Optional Hummus

    Recipe:

  1. Cook that fatty ass chicken. You probably want it marinated before, but like I said. White and know jack shit about schwarma
  2. Chop that bad boy up
  3. Spread your garlic sauce along the pita
  4. Lay your chicken on top of the garlic sauce
  5. Top with optional tomato and lettuce
  6. Serve along side hummus if you have it
  7. GOODBYE SCHWARMA
